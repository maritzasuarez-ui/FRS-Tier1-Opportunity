FRS A∧B∧C Rubric — Tag Reference

A contact resolves only when ALL THREE criteria pass simultaneously
A ∧ B ∧ C
The three-criteria rubric
A
Was the issue addressed?
PASS ✓
Expert engaged and provided guidance or a solution.
FAIL ✗
Issue acknowledged but not addressed, or call ended before expert could engage.
AND
B
Is anything still pending?
PASS ✓
No open dependencies — no pending Intuit action, customer action, or external process.
FAIL ✗
A blocker remains: follow-up needed, transfer required, or customer must still act.
AND
C
Did the customer understand the outcome?
PASS ✓
Customer confirmed understanding — acknowledged next steps or resolution.
FAIL ✗
No confirmation. Customer ended the call confused or without clarity.
Methodology notes
Primary tag by priority hierarchy. A contact failing both B and C gets one tag — whichever ranks highest in V2 hierarchy.

Judgment calls. customer_refuses_steps is B (path forward existed). transfer_recommended is B (contact didn't close) but can be overlay-credited for good transfers.

Overlay-credited tags. customer_action_required, multi_issue_flag_partial_res, good_transfer_recommended can be Resolved via overlay even though they fail B.
